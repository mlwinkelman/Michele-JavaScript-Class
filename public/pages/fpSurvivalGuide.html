Foster Parent Survival Guide

I designed the logo, color palette, branding, and sign-in screen for an iPhone app that's being developed as a centralized documentation and event tracker for foster parents. Being a foster parent can be chaotic and oftentimes overwhelming so the singular brand design goal was to project a sense of calmness and support. The polygonal background's significance is threefold, reflecting the multifaceted challenges the children in the system face, the complexities of caring for them, and the importance of maintaining the connections that are so essential to the process. The ribbon heart in the logo symbolizes support for a cause.
