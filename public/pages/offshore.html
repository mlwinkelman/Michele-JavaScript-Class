Offshore Water Sports

This was a branding and graphics package proposal for the launch of a new private label water sports line for a major sporting goods retailer. I was given just one week to create and develop the brand name, logo, color palette, photography options, hang tags, and retail marketing. This family-friendly brand would include both soft and hard line products for men, women, and kids. The brand positioning strategy was focused on performance fabrics & materials, value, and the aesthetic of an ‘aspirational’ surf brand such as Billabong, Roxy, etc. After researching and considering many different options for the brand name, I chose Offshore because it not only seemed instantly recognizable and connected to the water theme but also refers to an ‘offshore wind’ that makes for ideal surfing conditions.
