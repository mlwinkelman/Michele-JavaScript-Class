Great Tastes Cookbook

This cookbook was a collection of family recipes and a collaboration with my husband who is the primary chef in our household. I designed the layout, he wrote and edited, and we self-published it as a promotional piece and gift for friends and relatives. It was so well-received that we did an additional print run to fulfill requests for more copies. As an added bonus, we were thrilled to learn that a family friend's entry of our granola recipe was a grand-champion winner at the Nebraska State Fair.

Software used:
Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop
