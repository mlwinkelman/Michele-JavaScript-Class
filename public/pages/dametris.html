D’Ametri’s Salon

Inspired by the relaxing, spa-like atmosphere of their recently renovated salon space, I refreshed the branding for D’Ametri’s and redesigned their website. Their existing site was dated and the navigation was cumbersome so I wanted to create a clean, sophisticated, responsive site with a modern look that spoke to their brand and better connected their web presence with their new physical space. I worked with the client to reorganize and categorize the site’s information to provide a more optimal user experience. The updated design also creates a more unified, cohesive brand statement that provides online visitors with an inviting glimpse into the D’Ametri’s experience. Be sure to check out the Style Guide/Brand Proposal for this project, also in my design portfolio.
